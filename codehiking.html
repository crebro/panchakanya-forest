Preservation Proposal
Designating Habitat Zones
Planning for defining specific areas within the wildlife conservation areas or jungles to focus on terrestrial animal species, ensuring comprehensive coverage and efficient resource allocation.
Manual Traditional Census
We had the idea to commence with a conventional traditional census, employing time-honored methods of physical observation to enumerate the animal populace accurately at first so that we can get enough information to store in the database.
Digital Identification Techniques
For the identification of animal species, pins will be installed on each individual animal and their paw pattern will be stored as their unique identity in the database that will help in the further processes of developing a reliable census technology.

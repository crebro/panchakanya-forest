Panchakanya Community Forest is a community forest located in Budanilkantha, Kathmandu. It is a beautiful forest with a variety of flora and fauna. The forest is a popular destination for nature lovers and hikers. The forest is home to a variety of wildlife including leopards, deer, and monkeys. The forest is also home to a variety of bird species. The forest is a great place to go for a walk and enjoy the beauty of nature.
